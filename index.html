basic of football

The most important thing in football is simple football which follows the rules;

    Scan the area

    After getting the ball see the area around you and your teamates positioning and analize the probablity of movements

    Pass the ball

    After scanning pass pass the ball to teamate who has free man or pass through the defeners to the teamate who has good positioning
